Yoke Collective & Rubie Green

BLUE SCREEN OF BREATH

Blue Screen of Breath is a collaborative one night exhibition and performance at LUVA, by Yoke Collective and Rubie Green. In response to the dreaded blue screen of death, Yoke and Rubie present a network of audio and visual interfaces where live generated data is captured and regurgitated and lost.
