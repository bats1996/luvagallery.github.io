Blind Sale

LUVA is having a fundraiser!

Proceeds will keep this independent artist run space going for its second year, allowing us to continue our programme and platform a diverse range of contemporary emerging and established artists. A portion of the donations will also go to the First Love Foundation, a crisis relief charity and foodbank that we work with in Tower Hamlets.
