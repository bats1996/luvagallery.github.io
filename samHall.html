Sam Hall

THE BOYS DOODLE BOOK

“A BloBBy Britain , Mr BloBBy once described as a symbol for a nation gone soft in the head , in a post Brexit Britain and infantilised world characters such as Mr BloBBy become like cuddly horse men of the apocalypse , harbingers of chaos but also a much needed distraction “

Sam Hall is an artist based in Oxford, and graduated from Oxford Brookes University in 2018. Sam’s work explores the relationship between childhood,consumerism and fetish and considers the impact of this relationship in forming our adult identities.
